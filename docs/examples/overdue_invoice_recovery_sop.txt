Collections call procedure - Overdue invoice recovery (v3, Finance Dept)

1. The agent must greet the customer and confirm they are speaking with the account
   holder before any account details are discussed. Identity is confirmed with the
   invoice number or registered email.
2. Once verified, state the outstanding balance and the days overdue (from the
   billing system) and ask when payment can be expected.
3. If the customer disputes the invoice, do not argue; record the dispute reason and
   inform them the billing team will review within 5 business days.
4. If the customer cites financial hardship, offer the standard installment plan
   (3 monthly payments, no fee). Wording required by legal: "This arrangement does
   not affect your credit standing with us."
5. If the customer refuses to pay or requests no further contact, politely end the
   call and mark the account for escalation. Never threaten legal action.
6. A promise to pay by a specific date, an agreed installment plan, or immediate
   payment ends the call successfully. Thank the customer either way.
